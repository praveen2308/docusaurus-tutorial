Sales Force Integration

ShopIQ provides a lead scoring feature. This feature is used to predict the propensity of a customer to buy a certain product. This feature can also be used to push good leads to SalesForce CRM. As soon as a customer is identified as a good lead the details of this customer along with the product details is pushed to the SalesForce CRM. Then you sales team can followup with the right leads from the CRM itself.
