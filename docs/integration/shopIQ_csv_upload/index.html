Upload Files

For ShopIQ to provide the right insights we need certain datapoints about the store. These datapoints are not part of the session related Analytics. To achieve a 360 degreee view of the customer journey and buying pattern. The client should upload the following data to ShopIQ platform

Upload Settings

The section to upload files can be found under the "App Settings" section (left hand sidebar). This section has different tab for different data points that needs to be uploaded.

1. Upload Customer Data

A list of your customers with details like customerId, emailId, first name, last name etc... This data will be helpful for when we are tracking a customer online and want to relate the activities with customer. Along with this its important from lead scoring dashboard standpoint as well.

CSV Format :

2. Upload Catalog Data

A list of product in your current catalog to the platform. This important for us to track and identify products and activities which each customer is performing with each PDP page. The productId, pricing and other details are further used as part of propensity scoring.

3. Upload Marketing Investment Data

For the platform to do ROI analysis against each campaign we would need the clients to upload the investment made to each campaign. Based on these and the data collected from the website about the different activities done by various customers under each campaign ShoIQ then generates a final score. This score can help you understand which channel is helping you make more money and the ones which is going in loesses.
