App and Stream Creation

In this Lab we will learn how to build an App on IQLECT platform and also how to create a stream and upload data to stream.

Step 1 : App creation

  • Login to the platform using your credentials.

  • Navigate to the APPs page and click on NEW APP

  • Now we start the app creation wizard.

  • Enter the APP name and description, click on next.
  • In this step we need to create a stream. For ex - Product stream with product_id, product_name, brand, category as attributes. Click on SUBMIT.

Step 2 : App Submition

  • Click on DONE.
  • Your APP is created now and you will be redirected to the APP Settings page of your APP.
